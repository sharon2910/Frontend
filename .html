About us

Founded on Oct 10, 2018, Wilson Herbs has become well established among the true lovers of Natural Herbs. The brand stands for Affordable, Vegan & Cruelty-free Hair & Skincare made with Natural Ingredients. Handmade in Bangalore, India, the entire WILSON HERBS range is produced in small batches every week to maximize freshness.

The Founder

My interest in Natural- Vegan and chemical-free products for my hair and skin led me into making and trying them on myself, when I loved the results, I started making small batches and handed them to friends and family who saw amazing results just as much as I did. Which led me to open WILSON HERBS, which indeed has become the best-selling hair oil in my town. I also run with a lot of love for people who love to take care of their hair (The Natural Crown) And skin (The best foundation) you can wear for a natural you.
